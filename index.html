You should go to Tulum, Mexico!

Tulum has beautiful beaches, wonderful ruins to explore, and amazing food and drink! Looking for a place to swim? Choose between the Gulf of Mexico or any one of hundreds of Cenotes, or underground lakes, accessible by foot! A short drive will bring you to Chichen Itza where you can explore ruins dating back hundreds of years!

You should go to Paris, France!

Paris is majestic! Spend time exploring history in their many museums and appreciate the ancient architecture. Explore sites such as the Louvre, the Eiffel Tower, the Arc de Triomphe, the Palace of Versailles or take in the sights at one of the many cafes on teh River Seine.

You should go to the Serengeti!

While technically more of an ecosystem than a place, you can choose between visiting Tanzania and Kenya to explore the Serengeti which hosts the largest terrestrial mammal migration in the world! Take in views of approximately 70 karge nannak and 500 bird species spanning a huge variety of habitats. Looking for somewhere to relax? Try local hotels and restaurants in Nairobi!
